## How Ice Fishing works — wheel, odds and payouts

Ice Fishing is the first **speed game show** built by Evolution, released in August 2025. A virtual money wheel with 53 segments is spun in rapid rounds hosted live. Just before the wheel turns, random **wheel multipliers of up to 10x** may be dropped onto one or more segments. Leaf segments settle instantly, while the three Fish segments open a bonus game in which the presenter reels in a catch and the final multiplier is uncovered — reaching a ceiling of **5,000x** per round (a payout cap applies).

| Segment | Slots (of 53) | Chance per spin | What happens |
|---|---|---|---|
| Leaf 1 | 23 | 43.4 % | Pays 1:1 at once (times the wheel multiplier if one lands) |
| Leaf 2 | 23 | 43.4 % | Pays 1:1 at once (times the wheel multiplier if one lands) |
| Lil' Blues | 4 | 7.5 % | Fish Bonus — the bonus you'll see most often |
| Big Oranges | 2 | 3.8 % | Fish Bonus — middle-weight multipliers |
| Huge Reds | 1 | 1.9 % | Fish Bonus — the rarest, with the largest multipliers |

- Landing **any bonus**: 7 of the 53 segments ≈ **13.2 %**, which works out to roughly one bonus every 7–8 spins on average.
- **Huge Reds** is a 1-in-53 result — on average about once per 53 spins.
- Published **RTP** reaches **97.10 %** on Leaf bets; the bonus bets swing harder and carry a slightly lower return.

> Every number above is a **theoretical long-run average**. Spins do not have memory: a bonus that has been missing for a long streak is **not** somehow "owed". The counters and alerts exist to describe what has happened — never to hint at what will.
